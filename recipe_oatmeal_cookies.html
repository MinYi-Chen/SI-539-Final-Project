Healthy Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the mashed bananas and rolled oats until well combined.
  3. Fold in the chocolate chips and cinnamon.
  4. Scoop 1 tablespoon of dough for each cookie onto the baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the edges are golden brown. Let them cool before eating.
