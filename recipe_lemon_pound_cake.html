Lighter Lemon Pound Cake

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, oil, honey, eggs, lemon juice, and lemon zest.
  3. Gently fold the flour into the wet ingredients until just combined (do not overmix).
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top.
  5. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let it cool before slicing.
