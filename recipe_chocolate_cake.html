Guilt-Free Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease an 8-inch cake pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  3. Add the applesauce, almond milk, and maple syrup to the dry ingredients and whisk until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes. Let the cake cool completely before serving.
